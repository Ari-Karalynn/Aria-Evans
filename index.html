About Me

A passionate actor with a powerful tenor voice and extensive acting training. Aria is charismatic, unabashed, naturally curious, tenacious, and youthfully imaginative. They are currently pursuing their BAC in Theatre with a Specialization in Musical Theatre. Ari is drawn to works that explore complex social themes like queerness, but also to projects that inspire their inner child and cartoonish physicality.
